About Us

ABOUT ANMOL EDUCATION AND CHARITABLE TRUSTs

Anmol Education and Charitable Trust is non governmental organization working to provide education, good health to the children and skilling to empower the youth of Rajkot slums.

Anmol is started with the aim of providing school education to vulnerable children, good health to malnourished children and skilling to empowered the youth in slums of Ghanteshwar 25 Ft. quarters. We works closely with the stakeholders and communities involved.

Our Approch

Community-Centered Education: We work closely with local communities to understand their unique needs, challenges, and aspirations. Through collaborative efforts, we develop tailor-made educational programs that address specific issues and leverage local resources.

Holistic Learning: Our education initiatives go beyond traditional classroom teaching. We emphasize holistic learning experiences that encompass academic knowledge, practical skills, and socio-emotional development. By nurturing well-rounded individuals, we prepare them to thrive in diverse environments and contribute meaningfully to society.

Partners

Our success at Anmol is intricately tied to the support and collaboration of our partners and supporters.
