About Anmol Education and Charitable Trust

About Us

Anmol Education and Charitable Trust is non governmental organization working to provide education, good health to the children and skilling to empower the youth of Rajkot slums.

Anmol is started with the aim of providing school education to vulnerable children, good health to malnourished children and skilling to empowered the youth in slums of Ghanteshwar 25 Ft. quarters. We works closely with the stakeholders and communities involved.
